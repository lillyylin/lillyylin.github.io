Lilly Lin is a scientist, designer, and artist. She completed her BA in Psychology at the University of California, Los Angeles in 2019, with a specialization in computing. During her time at UCLA, she completed undergraduate research work in systems neuroscience with the Adhikari Lab, undertaking a variety of roles from experimental design, histology preparation and analysis, animal care and handling, and data analysis. She is currently working as a research associate in the Hamilton Lab at the University of California, San Diego. Outside of work, Lilly enjoys drawing and design. Lilly strives to create and implement creative solutions in all her work, both in and out of the lab. She intends to pursue her doctorate in the future.
